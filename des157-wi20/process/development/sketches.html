Final Project Sketches

Sketches Summary

After looking at lots of interactive websites, I have decided to do a story for my final project. I have not decided whether I want my website to scroll down or across the story, but I know that I want to split it up into three sections: "Research & Reports," "Stories," and "Take Action." In these sections, I want to explore different aspects of mental health among adolescents and provide a good case regarding why people should take action and incorporate mental health education into school curriculum.
