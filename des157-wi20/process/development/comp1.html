This is a rough outline of my website. The front page will be very simple, but eye-catching. If you click on any of the words on the front page, they will take you to that specific section, and then you can always click on the logo at the top to get back to the home page.

If you choose to follow the downward arrow and just keep scrolling, then you will get to the first section, "Research." Here there will be a clickable section (similar to studio 2) where you press on an individual mental health issue and a pop-up will give you more information. The next section is "Stories," which showcases stories from teenagers suffering with mental health issues in a slideshow. The viewer also gets the option to complete a form at the bottom of this section in order to share their own story. Lastly, the "Action" section shows steps you can take in order to help raise awareness about mental health.
